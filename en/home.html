Company

Digitalia Sistemi S.r.l. was founded in 2007 as an ITC consulting and software development ‘process-oriented’ company. With the experience gained by founders in more than twenty years in the Information Technology world, and the pool of specialists who work with the company, it is able to offer its Customers the skills and technologies needed to accomplish mission-critical ITC projects, characterizing itself by diligence, reliability, expertise and a strong Customer care culture.

Services

Our consultancy services, oriented to the development of information systems, cover the whole software development lifecycle, from user requirements gathering, through project organization, technical design up to implementation phases. Our services include on-site consultancy services, giving support to Customer’s working groups, as well as ‘turn-key’ projects that can be developed off-site, making use of our software-factory.
